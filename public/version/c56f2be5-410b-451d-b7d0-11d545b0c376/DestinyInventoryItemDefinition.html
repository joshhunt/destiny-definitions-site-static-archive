Captain's Coins are used as donations toward improvements in the Eliksni Quarter and can be exchanged at the Donations Chest in the Tower Courtyard. Captain's Coins can be earned by completing Ketchcrash and Expedition activities, opening chests, and completing Lost Sectors during Season of Plunder.
