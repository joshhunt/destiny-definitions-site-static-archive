• Earn Silver Leaves from completing activities throughout the game with Sunlit armor equipped. Wearing a full set of Sunlit armor rewards the most Silver Leaves. Launch into the Bonfire Bash and stoke the bonfire to turn Silver Leaves into Silver Ash. Doing so grants access to better Embers and stat-roll choices from Sparks. • Glows can be acquired for Sunlit armor by fully rekindling an armor piece by applying Kindling. • Solstice reward packages, which may contain Masterwork materials, can be purchased from Eva using Silver Ash.
